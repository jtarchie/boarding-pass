Welcome to the Concourse team!

Quick links:

  • Roadmap: project.concourse-ci.org

  • Docs: github.com/concourse/docs

This team may feel a bit different. Our customer is the open-source community, and there are many stakeholders: every software engineer on Earth should probably be using CI/CD.

Value is delivered not only through shipping features, but through engaging with the community and enabling community members to grow into contributors. For this reason there is greater emphasis on doing the right thing and being kind.

Concourse is a large project, so it may take a while before it feels like you're up to speed. This is a feeling that we're all familiar with. If you ever feel overwhelmed or just have questions don't hesitate to reach out to anyone in the Roles page or any of your teammates for support.

To make onboarding a bit easier, we divided the scope of the Concourse codebase into separate Tracks. Each track has a different subject matter and works with different languages and tools. You're free to rotate between tracks at any time, though you may want to try sticking with one for a while at first.
