2 Teams

2.1 Open-source team

  • Product Managers: Alex Suraci, Daniel de Repentigny

  • Designers: Lindsay Auchinachie, Matthew Periera

The Concourse OSS team ships Concourse to the open-source community and collaborates with contributors on GitHub.

2.2 Pivotal Concourse team

  • Product Manager: Scott Foerster

  • Designers: Lindsay Auchinachie, Matthew Periera

The Pivotal Concourse team ships supported versions of open-source Concourse to enterprise customers and builds on support relationships for customer feedback.

2.3 Duty Free team

  • Product Manager: Bishoy Youssef

  • Designers: Isha Bagha

The Duty Free team ships the "Resource Types" website (see Duty Free), where Concourse users can discover community-developed and supported resource types.
